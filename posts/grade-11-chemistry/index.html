Mixtures & Pure Substances

  • Pure substances cannot be broken down by physical means - only chemical means

  • Substances contain 2+ substances not joined together by chemical means

  • Mixtures:

    • Homogenous (solution) : Uniform appearance
    • Heterogeneous: Distinctly different substances can be seen (phases)

Pure Substances

  • Element
    • Atoms
    • Molecules (where 2+ atoms of the same element are bonded bonded together. E.g., O2)
  • Compound
    • Moleculues (where 2+ atoms of different elements are bonded together. E.g., CO)
